## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Volga Federal District** (Russian: Приволжский федеральный округ, 
> IPA: [prʲɪˈvolʂskʲɪj fʲɪdʲɪˈralʲnɨj ˈokrʊk]) is one of the eight federal districts of Russia. 
> 
> It forms the southeastern part of European Russia. 
> It is the second most populated federal district (after Central). 
> Its population was 29,899,699 (70.8% urban) according to the 2010 Census, 
> living on an area of 1,038,000 square kilometers (401,000 sq mi). 
> 
> Igor Komarov was appointed the federal district's Presidential Envoy 
> on 18 September 2018.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Volga%20Federal%20District)
