## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Southern Federal District** (Russian: Южный федеральный округ, 
> IPA: [ˈjuʐnɨj fʲɪdʲɪˈralʲnɨj ˈokrʊk]) is one of the eight federal districts of Russia. 
> 
> Its territory lies mostly on the Pontic–Caspian steppe of Southern Russia. 
> The Southern Federal District shares borders with Ukraine, the Azov Sea, 
> and the Black Sea in the west, and Kazakhstan and the Caspian Sea  in the east.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern%20Federal%20District)
