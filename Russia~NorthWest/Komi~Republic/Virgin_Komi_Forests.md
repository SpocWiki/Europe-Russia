## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Virgin Komi Forests (Komi: Комилӧн вӧрзьӧдлытӧм вӧръяс, 
> Russian: Де́вственные леса́ Ко́ми) is a natural UNESCO  World Heritage Site 
> in the Northern Ural Mountains of the Komi Republic, Russia. 
> 
> At 32,800 km2 it is the largest virgin forest in Europe.
>
