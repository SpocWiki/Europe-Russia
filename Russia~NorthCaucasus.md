## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **North Caucasian Federal District** (Russian: Северо-Кавказский федеральный округ, 
> IPA: [ˌsʲevʲɪrə kɐfˈkasːkʲɪj fʲɪdʲɪˈralʲnɨj ˈokrʊk]) 
> is one of the eight federal districts of Russia. 
> 
> It is located in extreme southern Russia, 
> in the geographical area of the North Caucasus. 
> 
> The federal district was split from the Southern Federal District on 19 January 2010. 
> 
> The population of the federal subjects comprising the federal district was 
> 10,171,434 according to the 2021 Census, 
> living in an area of 170,400 square kilometers (65,800 sq mi). 
> 
> The current Envoy is Yury Chaika.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North%20Caucasian%20Federal%20District)
